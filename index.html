The Problems

Now that we know what is there, here is what those findings reveal about what is getting left on the table during the rebrand.

Problem 1

The Rebrand Is Shipping Without a Conversion Layer

Your internal team is heads down on the platform rebuild right now. Platform rebuilds always consume the timeline. The piece that gets cut is the marketing layer: the lead magnet, the email gate, the nurture sequence, the retargeting setup. That is the exact layer that turns Resintel launch traffic into qualified trials.

Here is what the buyer journey looks like on launch day if nothing changes:

1

Procurement Manager Searches "Resin Pricing 2026"

A buyer at a mid-market plastics processor googles for PP or HDPE pricing trends. They land on the new Resintel homepage.

2

They Poke Around Resintel

They read about Resindex, click on the weekly Resinsights summary, and see the pricing charts. They are impressed but they are also comparing against OPIS/Chemical Market Analytics, S&P Global, and ICIS.

3

No Intermediate Step Exists

Resintel only has Start Complementary Trial and Book a Demo. Both are commitments. There is no download this report option for someone who is still researching. They are not ready for a trial but they would leave their email for something valuable.

4

They Leave and Never Come Back

The prospect bounces to an OPIS or S&P Global comparison, that competitor captures their email with a gated report, and they spend the next 30 days in that competitor's inbox. You do not.

Every visitor who leaves during the launch window without giving you their email is a Resintel trial your competitors get to nurture instead.
Problem 2

You Are Launching Into a Tracking Vacuum

Resintel loads PostHog and Axiom (good for product analytics) but the flagship Plastics Exchange domain has no Google Analytics, no Meta pixel, no LinkedIn Insight tag, and no email platform detected in page source. That means during the biggest commercial launch in years, most of your launch window traffic cannot be measured, attributed, or retargeted.

No Visitor Attribution

Without GA4 or GTM, you cannot tell which channel is driving Resintel trial signups, which blog posts are pulling organic traffic, or which partnership referrals actually land. The rebrand is a perfect moment to fix this.

No Retargeting Layer

Without a Meta pixel or LinkedIn Insight tag, every visitor who bounces is gone. You cannot run a retargeting sequence that says "you checked out Resindex, here is the weekly Resinsights report" to pull them back.

No Visible Nurture Infrastructure

A newsletter signup form exists but the platform behind it is not visible from the frontend. Before the rebrand ships, this is worth verifying. If the current newsletter is not tied to a welcome sequence or lead magnet delivery, the rebrand is the moment to connect it.

Every week the rebrand goes live without attribution, you launch blind into the most important traffic window this company has ever had.
Problem 3

A Direct Competitor Already Launched The Playbook

resinsmart.ai

ResinSmart, backed by Resin Technology Inc., launched in September 2025 running the exact self-serve acquisition funnel Resintel needs: free trial, weekly newsletter, ungated glossary, blog content aimed at plastics buyers who suspect they are overpaying for resin, and a clean demo booking path. While the legacy incumbents (OPIS, S&P Global, ICIS) stay locked in enterprise sales motions, ResinSmart has been quietly capturing the researcher traffic for seven months.

What ResinSmart Is Doing Right Now

Free basic tier with weekly market emails, a content library indexed for Google, and a newsletter funnel aimed at procurement buyers. Every month without a response, they compound authority in your category.

Why Resintel Still Wins If You Move Fast

You have the one asset ResinSmart cannot replicate: a 25-year spot marketplace with 10 to 20 million pounds of live inventory behind the data. A properly built lead magnet plus nurture sequence puts that moat in front of every researcher, not just the enterprise buyer willing to sit through a sales demo.

The rebrand is your moment to catch up on the self-serve layer and pull ahead on depth. Every month it ships without a lead capture funnel is a month ResinSmart keeps the research-stage buyers.
